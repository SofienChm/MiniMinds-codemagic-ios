1. Introduction

Welcome to MiniMinds ("we," "our," or "us"). We operate a daycare management platform that helps daycare centers, parents, and teachers manage daily activities, attendance, communications, and more.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and web platform (collectively, the "Service").

By using MiniMinds, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Service.

2. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Important Notice for Parents and Guardians

MiniMinds is designed to help daycare centers manage information about children. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

2.1 Information About Children

We collect information about children only as necessary to provide our daycare management services. This information is provided by:

  • Parents or legal guardians who register their children
  • Daycare administrators and teachers who manage daily activities

2.2 Types of Children's Information

Information collected about children may include:

  • Name and date of birth
  • Gender
  • Profile photo (optional)
  • Medical information (allergies, medical notes) for safety purposes
  • Daily activity records (meals, naps, activities)
  • Attendance records
  • Photos taken during daycare activities

2.3 Parental Consent

We require verifiable parental consent before collecting personal information about children under 13. Parents must:

  • Create an account and verify their identity
  • Explicitly consent to the collection of their child's information
  • Agree to our Terms of Service and this Privacy Policy

2.4 Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:

  • Review their child's personal information at any time through the app
  • Request deletion of their child's information
  • Refuse further collection of their child's information
  • Withdraw consent at any time by contacting us

2.5 Use of Children's Information

Children's information is used solely for:

  • Managing daycare activities and attendance
  • Communicating with parents about their child
  • Ensuring child safety (medical information)
  • Generating reports for parents and administrators

We do NOT:

  • Use children's information for marketing purposes
  • Share children's information with advertisers
  • Allow behavioral advertising based on children's data
  • Make children's information publicly available

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our Service
  • Manage user accounts and authentication
  • Track attendance and daily activities
  • Facilitate communication between parents and teachers
  • Process payments for daycare fees
  • Send notifications about your child's activities
  • Ensure child safety through medical information
  • Generate reports and analytics for administrators
  • Improve our Service based on usage patterns
  • Respond to support requests and inquiries
We do NOT use your information for:
  • Selling to third parties
  • Behavioral advertising
  • Profiling for marketing purposes

We may disclose your information:

6. Data Storage and Security

6.1 Where We Store Data

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure password hashing
  • JWT token-based authentication
  • Role-based access control
  • Regular security updates

6.2 Data Security Measures

  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel
  • All API communications are encrypted
  • Database access is protected and monitored
  • Regular security assessments are conducted
Important: While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

7.1 Access and Portability

You can view all your personal information within the app at any time.

7.2 Correction

You can update your profile and personal information through the app settings.

7.3 Deletion

You can request deletion of your account and all associated data:

  • In-App: Go to Settings > Delete Account
  • Email: Contact us at support@miniminds.com

Upon deletion request, we will permanently delete:

  • Your account and profile information
  • All messages and notifications
  • Activity history and comments
  • Device tokens and preferences

7.4 Opt-Out

You can opt out of:

  • Push notifications (through device settings)
  • Email notifications (through app settings)
  • Location tracking (through device settings)

7.5 Data Retention After Deletion

Some data may be retained for legal or legitimate business purposes:

  • Transaction records (for tax/legal compliance)
  • Anonymized analytics data
  • Data required by law to retain

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services:

Data Type Retention Period
Account Information Until account deletion
Activity Records Until account deletion
Messages Until account deletion
Payment Records 7 years (legal requirement)
Location Data Not stored permanently (used only for verification)

9. Cookies and Tracking

Our mobile app does not use cookies. Our web platform uses minimal cookies for:

  • Authentication (session management)
  • Language preferences

We do not use tracking cookies for advertising or analytics purposes.

10. Changes to This Policy

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending a notification through the app for material changes

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
